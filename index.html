Moulvibazar Half Marathon-2019

21.1 KM    |    7.5 KM

Half Marathon        Mini Marathon

22 NOVEMBER 2019

FRIDAY, 7 AM

Moulvibazar Half marathon is a unique opportunity for runners Both professionals and amateurs from around Bangladesh and the globe to run together and enjoy the beauty of the tea town of Bangladesh basically moulvibazar have a won unique beauty of Madhapkundo Waterfall, hum hum water fall and here around the tea garden and watery land like Haka luki and baiika bill and here live in various type of ethnic people along with the mainstream people . This running way is mainly flat and slide up hill makes it ideal for beginners , seasonal runners , family and friends.
